About Us

Eye See You

We are a group of high school sophomores at Thomas Jefferson High School for Science and Technology. We created the web application Eye See You as a way to help improve accessibility and convenience for diagnosing nearsightedness, the most common vision problem in the modern-day.

We were inspired to make this application because almost of all of our team members suffer from a nearsightnedness problem. Helping diagnose nearsightnedness early on is one of the best ways to prevent one's vision from getting worse, and by having a tool that is within one's reach allows for greater accessibility to eye prescription services. This application does not replace doctors, but serves as a way instead to help people make a judgement themselves to go to a doctor or not.
